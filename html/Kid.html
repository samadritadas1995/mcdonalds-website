Ronald McDonald made his first public appearance in Washington DC in 1963. In 1966, he debuted in the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade in New York City on November 25, 1966.

In 1967, McDonald’s Corporation named Ronald McDonald as its official spokesperson and ambassador to children. Following which, he appeared on US national television as the star of a McDonald’s commercial.
